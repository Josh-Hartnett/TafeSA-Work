About QTL

The Quality, Teaching and Learning directorate supports educational administrative teams in meeting quality and compliance requirements of the full range of VET and Higher Education regulatory frameworks.

Data Services

Data Services Team provide advice and support around integrity and Student Information Systems (SIS). The system Training Team facilitates SIS training, guides and forms.

Higher Education

Higher Education Services provides advice and support for TAFE SA and University Collaborations, development and accreditation of courses, and maintaining academic governance requirements.



Parchment Issuance

The Issuance Unit is learner centric focussed and supports Educational Service by providing a centralised process for the Issuance of valid certification.

Quality

The Quality Unit provides support to educational and administrative teams in meeting quality and compliance requirements.

Registrations

Registrations manages TAFE SA's registration with ASQA and Subsidised Training including provider communications with DSD and schedule vartions as well.
