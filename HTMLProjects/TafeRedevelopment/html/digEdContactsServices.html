Digital Education Consultancy Services for Educational Workgroups

Digital Education Consultants are able to combine project management, design and technical expertise to assist educational workgroups to develop learning resources, whether it's print or digital. Digital Education Consultants can provide advice and a range of models, tools, templates and planning strategies to ensure the content is appropriate for the intended delivery method and target audience.

The Unit provides solutions on enhancing learning resources including:

Coordination of digital learning resource development projects

Learning object development

Educational design of digital teaching & learning resources

Storyboarding advice & learning activity design of digital objects

Instructional design for online & digital resources

Copyright, IP & commercialisation advice on learning resources

Graphic design for printed learning resources

Advanced TAFE SA Learn (Moodle) technical advice

Design & authoring of commercial quality self-paced online courses using captivate software

iBook/ePub publishing, video & audio post-production, graphic design & template design

Digital Capability Framework

The TAFE SA Digital Capabilities Framework comprises a student profile and a staff profile. It has been adpated by the JISC (UK).

Student Profile (Digital Learning)

Digital learning centres on the enablement of learners throughout the lifecycle of their education, to engage with educational staff and learning materials from anywehre, at any time and ina range of modes. Technologies may be used to enhance face to face classrom experiences or to provide a mechanism tha tallows learners to progress at their own pace and under their own direction.


Staff Profile (Digital Teaching)

Digital teaching focuses on the fit for purpose digital tools and technologies that empower lecturers to provide high quality educaiton to all learners. Digital teaching may encompass greater flexibility in the delivery mode, be delivered from a range of locations or at a time which is more suitable to the learner. Digital learning and Digital Teaching have close relationships to other areas of the Digital Business framework, particularly Digital Student Operations such as admissions, timetabling and customer service.


Tafe SA Learn Course Template

The DEU has been working closely with a group of Lecturers piloting what will become the standard TAFE SA Learn Course Template for every unit of competency delivered online. The template will provide consistent look and feel across all qualificaitons, standardising navigation, course content, activities, resources and assessment components.

The pilot group has commenced the trial in August 2018 and the DEU is collecting feedback to ensure the final product is aligned with the Digital Strategy, Course Standards and Delivery Methodology. The Course Template can only be accessed by the people selected for the pilot and will not be distributed until the final product meets the business requirements.

Tafe SA Digital Education Standards

The DEU has licensed the internationally recognised Quality Matters Standards for online delivery. Based on extensive academic research, the Quality Matters standards will be adapted to meet the VET sector of requirements as well as Australian Government Regulators.


TAFE SA Community of Digital Practice

The Digital Education Network (DEN) is a key structural component that will enable all elements of TAFE SA's Digital Education Strategy. Our aim is to enhance the experience of learners and the role of educational staff in line with the changing needs of our students and industry clients.

Purpose of the Business Unit DEN:

Develop & implement creative educational design & delivery methods

Add value to the relationships between the lecturer & learner

Create learning experiences that engage learners & create a high level of satisfaction

Share models of the best practice & emerging technologies & methodologies

Increase the digital capability of our staff to consistently deliver high-quality education & training

Ensure consistency of Practice in Digital Learning throughout the BU & TAFE SA

Receive & relay information about strategic Digital Learning developments including the "Digital Education Straegy & the "Roadmap"

Provide feedback via the Digital Educators Leadership Network (DELN) in terms of "what's working" & "what's not"
