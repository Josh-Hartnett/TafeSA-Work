Change Management Process

The Digital Education transformation review is held in line as per the TAFE SA Change Management Proccess and line with relevant Enterprise Agreements. Hover over the images below to see our change management process.

Consultation

(10 working days): initiates consultation with relevant employees and outlines the intention and purpose of the review.

Recommendation

Feedback is considered and final organisational structure is identified and provided to all employees.

Implementation

Employees are directly assigned into the new structure, and if required, selection processes are run, using the relevant process.

Employees who do not transition to the new structure are assisted to find alternate employyment or may be offered a TVSP.

Questions & Answers


Please ask any questions you may have regarding the Digital Education Transformation review via the Q&A portal here or e-mail your information to QTL@tafesa.edu.au. You can provide your name or ask the question anonymously but please remember questions and answers may be psoted below and all staff are able to view this page so if the question is of a personal nature it may be better to email your manager.

We will endeavour to response to questions asked in relation to the proposed structure within 24 hours. Feedback and suggestions regarding the proposal will be consolidated and presented holistically after consultation has closed. Please note that only feedback relevant to the proposed structure, roles within it and the process to be followed from this point forward will be published and responded to.
